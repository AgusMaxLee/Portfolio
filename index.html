About Me

Hey! I'm Agustin, a game programmer and creative developer studying Computational Arts at Concordia University. I started out with a DEC in Computer Science but the creative side of things is what really gets me going.

I love making games because it brings everything I care about into one place. Music, art, animation, code, design. It all fits. I work mostly in Unity and Unreal Engine 5 but I also build 3D web environments with Three.js, which has been a really exciting side of what I do.

I'm a team person. Some of my favorite moments making games have been in a room with people who are all excited about the same idea and just building together. I'm looking for opportunities where I can keep getting better, work on real projects and make stuff I'm genuinely proud of.
