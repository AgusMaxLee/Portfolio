Survive Sim

Gameplay Programmer & Level Designer

Unreal Engine 5 FP Puzzle / Survival In Progress

Project Overview

A 3D first-person puzzle/survival game currently in development. The player controls a character in a real-world 3D environment, but they also have access to a 2D companion character that lives inside a computer monitor. Gameplay revolves around switching between the two perspectives — using the 2D character to solve puzzles on-screen while staying alert in the 3D world where monsters and threats can approach at any time.

The dual-perspective mechanic creates a constant tension between focused puzzle-solving and situational awareness, making every moment feel high-stakes.

My Contributions

  • Implemented the character-switching system that lets the player transition between the 3D and 2D perspectives.
  • Developed the in-game monitor display that renders the 2D character's world within the 3D environment.
