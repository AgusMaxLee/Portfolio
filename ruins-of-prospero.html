Ruins of Prospero

Gameplay Engineer & Game Designer

Unity 3rd Person RPG Team Project

Project Overview

A third-person RPG set in a low-poly fantasy world where the player controls a wizard capable of switching between four elemental modes: water, fire, earth, and sword-only. Each element grants unique abilities and passive effects that alter the player's HP, mana, and damage output — tying into the game's central theme of "side effects." Players must defeat three elemental bosses across an open-world environment while managing their element choices strategically, fighting off small ghost enemies, and collecting apples to restore health.

My Contributions

  • Developed the full character movement system and all associated animations.
  • Implemented the elemental switching system (water, fire, earth, sword) with unique abilities per mode.
  • Built the stat-modifying mechanic where changing elements dynamically affects HP, mana, and damage.
  • Enhanced the AI logic for all three main elemental bosses.
  • Populated the open-world map with ghost enemy patrols and attack behaviors.
  • Designed and implemented all in-game UI elements (HUD, menus, health/mana bars).
  • Integrated the apple health-recovery system.
