Ruins of Prospero

Gameplay Engineer & Game Designer

Unity 3rd Person RPG Team Project

Project Overview

This was my biggest Unity project and honestly one of the most fun to work on. It's a third-person RPG in a low-poly world where you play as a wizard who can switch between water, fire, earth and sword mode. The cool part is that switching elements doesn't just change your abilities, it also changes your HP and mana. So you're always making tradeoffs. There are three elemental bosses to defeat and a whole open world to explore.

What I worked on

  • Built the whole character movement system and all the animations.
  • Made the elemental switching system with unique abilities for each mode.
  • Built the mechanic where your stats actually change depending on which element you're using.
  • Improved the AI on the three main bosses.
  • Populated the open world with ghost enemies.
  • Did all the UI, HUD, health bars, mana bars and menus.
  • Added the apple healing system.
