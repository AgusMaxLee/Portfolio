Sofi is Sick

Solo Developer — Built in 24 Hours

Unity 2D Platformer / FPS Solo Project

Project Overview

This was a personal challenge. I gave myself 24 hours to go from nothing to a finished, playable game. It starts as a 2D platformer and then halfway through it switches into a first person shooter out of nowhere. The whole tone shifts completely and that transition was the fun part to pull off.

What I worked on

  • Did all the 2D platformer mechanics, movement, obstacles and progression.
  • Built the transition from 2D platformer into the first person shooter section.
  • Put together all the assets and dialogue my brother wrote.
  • The whole thing from idea to finished game in under 24 hours, solo.

Tech Stack

Unity C# Physics2D
