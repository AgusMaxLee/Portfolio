Sofi is Sick

Solo Developer — Built in 24 Hours

Unity 2D Platformer / FPS Solo Project

Project Overview

A surprise gift for my cousin — built solo in 24 hours. The game starts as a calm, lighthearted 2D platformer before suddenly switching gears into a chaotic first-person shooter. The genre twist is played for laughs, turning a peaceful experience into something absurd and intense. My brother helped write the dialogue, adding to the personal, one-of-a-kind feel of the project.

It's one of my favorite things I've made — not because of its scale, but because of what it represents: the ability to take a creative idea from concept to playable game in a single day.

My Contributions

  • Designed and programmed all 2D platformer mechanics: movement, jumping, obstacles, and progression.
  • Built the genre-switch transition from 2D platformer to 3D first-person perspective.
  • Integrated all artistic assets and dialogue written by my brother.
  • Managed the full solo development pipeline from concept to delivery in under 24 hours.
