Team LATAM

Game Programmer | Gameplay Engineer | Game Designer

Game Engine: Unity

Project Overview

A fun 2D platformer that unexpectedly evolves into a first-person shooter. The game starts off with a calm and relaxing atmosphere but changes into a more sinister and intense scene, adding an element of surprise and comedy. This was a 24-hour solo development project, with a little help from your brother for the dialogues, made as a birthday present for your cousin.

Key Features I Worked On

  • I was responsible for all the mechanical aspects of the game except for the artistic assets and the shooter mechanics and animations in the final part of the game.
